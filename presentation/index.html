STEP 01 / CAPTURE — ZERO TYPING

She photographs it, or just speaks it.

A page of her bahi-khata, a customer's bill, or a wholesaler challan — Claude Opus 4.8 vision reads the Hindi/Hinglish handwriting; a fine-tuned whisper.cpp Hindi model handles voice on-device. Capture is mode-aware: a daily scribble becomes ledger entries, a customer bill posts to that customer's udhaar, a challan becomes priced stock. No forms, no jargon.

STEP 02 / UNDERSTAND

Paper becomes a live ledger.

Entries auto-build a daily P&L (sales, COGS, net, cash vs udhaar), an inventory catalogue (stock, cost→sell margin, low-stock alerts) and an udhaar tracker. Every sale line snapshots its price & cost, so margins stay accurate even when catalogue prices drift — credit-grade data, not estimates.

STEP 03 / ADVISE

It stops being a ledger.

Four AI agents — Demand, Margin, Competition, Strategy — brief her daily. On top sits an agentic assistant: she asks anything in her own language and a tool-calling model queries her live data and answers in Hinglish with inline charts — what's selling, who owes most, what to reorder. It can also turn a spoken sentence into a sale or repayment she confirms.

STEP 04 / SELL

One tap — "Go online."

Her live inventory publishes as a neighbourhood storefront (delivery / pick-on-arrival). Orders land on an owner page she opens inside the app. Built from data she already has, it's her counter-punch to quick commerce.

STEP 05 / CREDIT

The asset she never had.

Every recorded rupee feeds an Artha Score (0–100) + an indicative working-capital band — an alternative-data credit profile from real commerce flow (sales consistency, margin, cost control, udhaar recovery). The asset a bank could never see on paper. NBFC-partnered origination is the roadmap and the business model.

RESILIENCE · CAPTURE NEVER FAILS

Works even when
the page doesn't.

Illegible handwriting, a blurry photo, a torn page — the camera is the fast lane, never the only one. Three ways in, all landing in the same ledger.

📷

Photo

Opus 4.8 vision reads the page. Unsure fields are flagged, not guessed — and a blurry shot prompts a retake instead of a wrong number.

🎙️

Speak

If the page won't read, she just says it. On-device whisper.cpp (fine-tuned Hindi) transcribes — fully offline.

⌨️

Type

The universal fallback — a sentence in her own words. Same parser, same ledger, zero dead ends.

ALL THREE FEED → ONE LEDGER
✓

She confirms every entry before it saves. Each capture lands on an editable review card, anchored to her own catalogue & customers — so even shorthand resolves to the right item and party. The model never gets the last word. She does.

TRUST · WHY SHE'LL ACTUALLY ADOPT IT

Not the system's tool.
Hers.

Shops run on cash to stay invisible — so "record every sale" can feel like filing an ITR: exposure, scrutiny, nothing back. Artha is the opposite, by design.

अपना

On her phone, not a server

No account, no upload, no cloud ledger. The data lives on the device — the cloud is touched only to OCR an image. Yeh aapka private bahi-khata hai — kahin nahi jaata.

फ़ायदा

It pays her back today

An ITR gives the shopkeeper nothing. Artha hands her udhaar she can collect this week, her real profit, and low-stock alerts — the value is hers, immediately.

मर्ज़ी

Credit is opt-in

The same record that feels like exposure is the asset that finally earns her a loan. Consent-based — she decides if and when to show her score to a lender.

No login · no KYC to start · works offline · in her language — every reason to say no, removed before we ask for any trust.
